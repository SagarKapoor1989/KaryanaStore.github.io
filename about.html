About us

कaryanaStore, a perfect combination of department and grocery store. ‘Value’ is a concept we take very seriously. And by ‘Value’ we mean great everyday prices on all of our products. Most grocers charge their suppliers fees for putting an item on the shelf which results in higher prices so we don’t do it. We keep our costs low – because every penny we save is a penny you save.

WE JUST FOCUS ON WHAT MATTERS:

GREAT PRODUCTS + GREAT PRICES = ‘VALUE’
